About Us

The Bihar Swimming Association (SFB), established in 1975, is the governing body for aquatic sports in Bihar, affiliated with the Swimming Federation of India.

Office Bearers

  • President: Mr. Rajesh Kumar
  • Secretary: Ms. Anjali Sharma
  • Treasurer: Mr. Sanjay Verma
  • Vice President: Dr. Meera Patel

Our Mission

To promote and develop swimming and aquatic sports in Bihar, identify and nurture talent, and represent the state at national and international levels.
